There are two main types of AI

Type 1 (More like machine and powerful)
Type 2 (More like a human like being with self-awareness and reactiveness)

Type 1:
a. Narrow AI (Weak AI) [Dedicated for some tasks]
b. General AI [Performs tasks like humans]
c. Strong AI [Super Humans] (Far future)

Type 2:
a. Reactive Machines [Only focus on current scenarios, do not store memory]
b. Limited Memory [Self-training and decision making algorithms]
c. Theory of Mind [Able to understand human emotions, and act like a social being]
d. Self Awareness [Having their own consciousness]


Communicative AI:

1. Interactive AI
2. Text AI
3. Functional AI 
4. Analytic AI
5. Visual AI
